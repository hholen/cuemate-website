Keyboard-first

No mouse needed
during your call

Play, pause, adjust speed, switch modes. Every action has a hotkey. Press T and your mouse passes right through to the window below.

Space Play / pause scroll ↑ ↓ Adjust speed N Switch to notes mode T Click-through toggle O Open a file Esc Expand / collapse

Notes mode

Static pages for
longer meetings

Not every meeting needs a teleprompter. Switch to notes mode for static reference pages you flip through with arrow keys. Use --- in your markdown as page breaks. Show pages on the left, right, or both sides.
